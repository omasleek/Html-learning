Delicious Nigerian Menu

Appetizers

  • Puff Puff

    Fried dough balls sprinkled with sugar, typically served as a snack or appetizer. Crunchy on the outside and soft on the inside.

  • Chicken Suya

    Thinly sliced pieces of Chicken marinated in a mixture of spices, peanut sauce, and other ingredients, then grilled over an open flame.

  • Akara

    Fried bean cakes made from mashed beans, onions, and spices. Crispy on the outside and fluffy on the inside, often served with a side of pepper sauce or tomato sauce. .

Main Courses

  • Jellof Rice and Chicken

    A classic Nigerian dish made with flavorful one-pot rice, tomatoes, onions, and spices, served with grilled or fried chicken.

  • Egusi Soup and Fufu

    A hearty soup made with ground melon seeds, vegetables, and meat or fish, served with a side of fufu (boiled and pounded cassava or yam).

  • Suya Beef with Yam Fries

    Thinly sliced beef marinated in a mixture of spices, peanut sauce, and other ingredients, then grilled over an open flame, served with crispy yam fries.

Desserts

  • Kunnu Aya

    A sweet and creamy dessert made from tiger nuts, milk, and sugar, similar to a pudding.

  • Groundnut Cake

    A sweet and nutty cake made with groundnuts (peanuts), sugar, and spices, often served as a snack or dessert.

  • Plantain Pudding

    A rich and creamy dessert made with ripe plantains, sugar, and spices, similar to a custard pudding.
